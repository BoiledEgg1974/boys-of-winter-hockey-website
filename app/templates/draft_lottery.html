Draft lottery

Two weighted draws (17th–24th group). Ball counts scale by finish: 17th place gets 8 balls, 18th gets 16, … 24th gets 64 (8× the slot number). The drum spins slowly with long radial paddles. Use Release balls and Open hatch on the overlay at the bottom of the drum. After release, open the hatch to show the yellow win line — first ball to touch that line wins that draw. Each winner moves up by up to five draft spots total across both draws; teams passed drop one spot per pass, and no team may fall more than two spots below where they started. Entertainment only — not an official league random draw.
