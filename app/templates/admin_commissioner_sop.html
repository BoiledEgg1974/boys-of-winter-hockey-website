Commissioner SOP (Beginner)

← Back to Site admin

This is your simple routine for running Site admin safely.

Daily (5-10 minutes)

  • Open Analytics & alerting dashboard and check for red/yellow alerts.
  • Open GM operations queue and process pending requests.
  • Open AP redemption requests and approve/deny pending items.
  • Open News Moderation to approve content, or use post article to publish.
  • Open Discord integration and confirm health checks are green.

Every 2-3 days

  • Review Announcement banners and remove old notices.
  • Review Homepage modules to confirm layout and visibility still look correct.
  • Review Franchise health dashboard for inactive/risky teams.
  • Check Audit log for unexpected high-impact actions.

Weekly

  • Review League rules and revert temporary settings you no longer need.
  • Review Redemption catalog and clean up stale reward items.
  • Review Manual AP ledger for corrections and unusual entries.
  • Review Story automation for scheduled jobs, retries, and errors.
  • In Discord integration, replay or requeue any dead-letter events.

Before major operations (important)

  • Use League control center dry-run/preview first.
  • Confirm backup exists before execute/restore/rollover actions.
  • Run one high-impact action at a time, then verify results.
  • Check Audit log after each major action.

High-risk tools (use carefully)

  • League control center (restore/import/rollover/execute operations)
  • Admin roles
  • League rules
  • Edit player contract (cap)
  • Undo center

Safety checklist (every time)

  • Confirm you are in the correct league site.
  • Read the action details twice before submitting.
  • Use preview/dry-run when available.
  • Ensure a backup exists before high-impact actions.
  • Verify the result immediately after the action.
